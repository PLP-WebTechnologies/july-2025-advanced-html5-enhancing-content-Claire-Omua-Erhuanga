Morning Market Prep Routine (5 Steps)

  1. Make a list of fresh items to buy.
  2. Sort your shopping bag and cash.
  3. Leave early to get fresh produce.
  4. Bargain and select quality ingredients.
  5. Return and clean the items for storage.
